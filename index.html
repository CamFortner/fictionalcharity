Our charity is called, "Broke Teacher". We help provide money for teachers so they don't have to use their hard earned money to buy school supplies for students or for their classrooms. For this charity, you can donate as much as you want. Every little bit helps.

How It Works

We'll ask the teachers to come up with a list of the supplies needed for the school year. Once they're done with the list, we buy the supplies from the money donated, and gift them to the teachers. This prevents them from using their hard earned money. Teachers need your support now more than ever.
